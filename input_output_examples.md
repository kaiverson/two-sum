# Input/Output Examples for Two Sum Problem

## Example 1:
### Input:
Array: [2, 7, 11, 15]
Target: 9
### Output:
Indices of numbers that add up to the target: [0, 1]

## Example 2:
### Input:
Array: [3, 2, 4]
Target: 6
### Output:
Indices of numbers that add up to the target: [1, 2]

## Example 3:
### Input:
Array: [3, 3]
Target: 6
### Output:
Indices of numbers that add up to the target: [0, 1]
